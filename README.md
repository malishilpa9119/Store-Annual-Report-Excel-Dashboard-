# Store Annual Report Excel Dashboard  

This Excel dashboard provides an end-to-end analysis of **store sales performance** using PivotTables, PivotCharts, and Slicers.  
It highlights **monthly trends**, **customer patterns**, **order status performance**, and **top regions & channels**, enabling data-driven decisions.

##  Dashboard Overview  

### **Key Insights**
- **Women contribute 64%** of total sales  
- **Delivery success rate: 92%**  
- **Maharashtra** generates the highest revenue  
- **Amazon** contributes **35%** of total channel sales  
- Adults (30–49) form the **largest buying segment**  
- Sales peak during **March & August**

## Dashboard Sections  

### **1. Sales vs Orders (Monthly Trend)**
Combo chart displaying:
- Bars → **Sum of Amount (Sales)**
- Line → **Count of Orders** on secondary axis  
Reveals monthly sales seasonality and demand patterns.


### **2. Sales: Men vs Women**
Pie chart representing:
- Women: **64%**
- Men: **36%**  
Shows female customers contribute majority of revenue.


### **3. Order Status Breakdown**
Pie chart showing order outcomes:
- Delivered (92%)  
- Cancelled (3%)  
- Returned (3%)  
- Refunded (2%)  


### **4. Top 5 States by Sales**
Bar chart ranking states:
- Maharashtra  
- Karnataka  
- Uttar Pradesh  
- Telangana  
- Tamil Nadu  


### **5. Age Group vs Gender**
Clustered bar chart comparing buying behavior of:
- Teen  
- Young Adult  
- Adult  
- Senior  
Across **Men** and **Women**.


### **6. Orders by Channel**
Pie chart showing contribution from:
- Amazon (35%)  
- Myntra (23%)  
- Flipkart (21%)  
- Meesho, Ajio, Others  


### **7. Interactive Slicer Panel**
Slicers added for:
- **Category**
- **Channel**
- **Month**

These slicers filter all dashboard charts dynamically.
